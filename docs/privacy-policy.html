Privacy Policy

App: Subscription Tracker
Last updated: February 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Subscription Tracker (“we”, “us”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application (the “App”).

Information We Collect

  • Account information: If you sign in with Google, we receive basic profile information such as your name, email address, and profile photo (as provided by Google).
  • Subscription and bill data: Information you enter in the App (e.g., subscription name, amount, billing frequency, due date).
  • Notifications data: Device tokens used to deliver push notifications (e.g., Firebase Cloud Messaging token).
  • SMS and device permissions (optional): If you grant SMS permissions, the App may read incoming SMS messages to detect bills/subscriptions and create bill entries. If you do not grant these permissions, this feature will not work.
  • App analytics: We may collect usage analytics through services like Firebase Analytics to understand App usage and improve reliability.

How We Use Information

  • Provide core App features (tracking subscriptions/bills, reminders, notifications).
  • Authenticate you and associate your data with your account.
  • Improve performance, fix bugs, and enhance features.
  • Prevent abuse and secure our services.

How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Service providers that help run the App, such as:
    • Google Firebase (Authentication, Cloud Messaging, Analytics)
    • Our backend hosting provider and database provider
  • Legal: if required to comply with law, enforce our terms, or protect users and our services.

Data Retention

We retain information for as long as necessary to provide the App and for legitimate business purposes (e.g., security, compliance). You may request deletion of your account data by contacting us.

Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Your Choices

  • You can sign out of the App at any time.
  • You can disable permissions (such as SMS) in your device settings, but features may stop working.
